"College mentorship with freedom and ease"

About:

UniVerse, developed by Stanford students, revolutionizes college counseling by breaking away from the rigid, traditional structure of existing services that often emphasize "traditional" target schools like Ivy Leagues. The app fosters peer-to-peer connections and empowers counselors with the freedom to craft their own unique advice, providing students with personalized, diverse insights and opening up a broader range of opportunities.
